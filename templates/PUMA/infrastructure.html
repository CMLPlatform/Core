Summary

In infrastructure we include all items facilitating transport of people and goods. The list of objects is inspired on Zhang et al. 2011, Wang and Graedel 2010, Van Beers & Graedel 2003 and Rauch et al 2007. Data on amounts of these items and their average lifetimes was estimated from this literature, annual reports, online municipality data, communication with experts. Metro, train, tram and highway related district data is based on own measurements from a map. For other product the amount per district is based on inhabitants.
