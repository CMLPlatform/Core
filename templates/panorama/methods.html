Methods

This report presents the methodology and first prototype of the Physical AccouNts Of RAw MAterial stock and flow Information Service (PANORAMA) database. The PANORAMA database seeks to combine extensive data on material flows and stocks and that will be the basis for a web-based information service system to assess current and future bottlenecks in the supply chain related to (critical) raw materials, specifically those that are currently not in the scope of existing tools and databases.

Modules

The model to generate the PANORAMA database is based on several modules.

  • Composition module
    This module assigns material and elemental composition of products, which are harmonized according to various uncertainty scores.

  • Classification module
    This module generates the Panorama classification (PC) system based on the selected elements and materials and the correspondence tables with other classification systems containing the raw data.

  • Data Module
    Here the raw data is harvested, pre-processed, harmonized, and assigned a level of uncertainty. Final data gaps in the supply-use tables are imputed by disaggregating hybrid Exiobase tables and balanced according to the uncertainty criteria.
